# KNOWLEDGE BASE DESIGN (IMPORTANT)

The Knowledge Base is one of the core components of E-Aspri.

Its purpose is not only document storage, but to create an AI assistant that understands the working context, writing style, planning documents, and administrative workflows commonly used by executive assistants and planning agencies.

The AI should prioritize answers based on uploaded organizational documents before relying on general knowledge.

---

## Knowledge Base Categories

### 1. Personal Working Documents

Used to learn the user's working style and frequently used document formats.

Examples:

* Previous speeches
* Previous remarks and opening statements
* Meeting notes
* Briefing notes
* Executive summaries
* Previous reports
* Previous TOR documents
* Previous official letters
* Previous internal memos

AI should learn:

* writing style
* document structure
* preferred wording
* commonly used phrases

---

### 2. Official Administrative Templates

Used as references when generating new documents.

Examples:

* Official letter templates
* Invitation letter templates
* Internal memo templates
* Nota Dinas templates
* Disposition templates
* Meeting invitation templates

AI should generate documents using the same formatting and structure found in uploaded templates.

---

### 3. Planning and Development Documents

Used to provide planning context.

Examples:

* RPJMD
* RKPD
* Renstra
* Renja
* Strategic planning documents
* Regional development plans
* Development priorities

AI should use these documents when answering planning-related questions.

Examples:

* Generate speeches aligned with development priorities.
* Create briefing notes based on RKPD.
* Explain development targets from planning documents.

---

### 4. Monitoring and Evaluation Documents

Examples:

* Monitoring reports
* Evaluation reports
* Quarterly reports
* Semester reports
* Annual reports
* Performance reports

AI should be able to:

* summarize reports
* identify key achievements
* identify issues and bottlenecks
* generate executive summaries

---

### 5. Meeting and Coordination Documents

Examples:

* Meeting minutes
* Meeting transcripts
* Coordination meeting reports
* Follow-up reports

AI should automatically extract:

* key decisions
* action items
* responsible persons
* deadlines

---

### 6. Musrenbang and Proposal Documents

Examples:

* Musrenbang reports
* OPD proposals
* Program proposals
* Development proposals

AI should be able to:

* summarize proposals
* identify priorities
* compare proposals
* generate briefing notes

---

### 7. Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

Examples:

* Internal SOPs
* Administrative SOPs
* Planning procedures
* Coordination procedures

AI should answer operational questions using SOP references whenever available.

---

## AI Knowledge Priority

When generating responses, prioritize information using the following hierarchy:

1. Uploaded personal documents
2. Uploaded organizational documents
3. Uploaded planning documents
4. Uploaded SOP documents
5. General AI knowledge

This ensures outputs remain aligned with actual organizational practices.

---

## Smart Actions for Every Uploaded Document

Every uploaded document should support:

* Summarize
* Extract key points
* Generate executive summary
* Create briefing note
* Generate meeting minutes
* Extract action items
* Generate presentation outline
* Generate speech points
* Generate follow-up checklist
